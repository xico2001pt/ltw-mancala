Board

  • Each player controls the holes on their side and the deposit to their right.

Playing

  • Each turn, the player removes all seeds from a hole, sowing the seeds.

Sowing

  • Sowing consists in placing one seed in each of the following holes in sequence, counter-clockwise;
  • If the next hole is the player's deposit, place a seed there too;
  • If the next hole is the adversary's deposit, don't place a seed there;
  • Keep sowing on the player's side if there are still seeds;
  • If the player's last seed was placed in the player's deposit, they get to play again;
  • If the player's last seed was placed in a player's empty hole, transfer the seeds on the adversary's oposite hole and and the player's last seed to the player's deposit.

End

  • The game finishes when one of the players can't make a move because all of its holes are empty;
  • The other player collects the seeds on its holes and places them on the deposit;
  • The player with more seeds on their deposit wins the game.
